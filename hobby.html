Arbor Axe House

An axe throwing bar in the heart of downtown Lincoln, Arbor Axe House is dedicated to building up the community and supporting local businesses. Owners Connor Pedersen, Madison Jurgens, Cecelia Orwig, and Cameron Warnke took over the space on September 2nd, 2025. Since then, they have revitalized the axe throwing scene in Lincoln with community partnerships and a welcoming space to hang out. Arbor Axe House strives to be a much-needed third-space in the Lincoln community.

"With Connor at the helm, relationships that we believed were three years down the road have become a reality within the first four months."
- Cecelia Orwig

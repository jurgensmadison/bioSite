Who is Connor Pedersen?

Connor Pedersen is a local business owner and pillar of the community. He works at the University of Nebraska - Lincoln and owns Arbor Axe House. His dedication to the community is evident in the many charity events he has organized, including the Fight For Mom charity tournament in 2021 and the annual Pup Crawl. While Connor is a Nebraska transplant, he has made Lincoln his home.

Family Life

Connor was born and raised in White Bear Lake, Minnesota. His parents, Steve and Lisa Pedersen, raised him alongside his older brother, Logan, and younger brother, Ryan. Connor moved to Lincoln to attend the University of Nebraska - Lincoln, where he became involved in Crew, Ballroom dance, and volunteering. After moving to New Mexico for an internship opportunity, he quickly returned and began working at the University.

Connor is engaged to Madison Jurgens. They have two adorable animals: a rescue husky mix named Po and a surprise kitten named Artemis. Their lives consist of chasing their dog, running their business, and planning their wedding.
